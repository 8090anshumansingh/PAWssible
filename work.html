Content Department

Our team of content creators is all about creativity and giving words to our thoughts. They help us in creating compelling content to evoke sensitivity in people towards animals.

Responsibilities:

  1. Writing informative Blogs, Articles, Newsletters for our website.
  2. Proofread the content to ensure that it is top-notch and error free.

Event Department

Our team of event organisers with a "can-do" attitude assist our organization in hosting fundraiser events to support our cause and ensure help to every furry friend.

Responsibilities:

  1. Organising online and offline events
  2. Brainstorming event plans and concepts.

Technical Department

Our Digital Marketing team, plans, implements , manages and monitors the Social Media handles of our organisation to help us spread awareness to a large audience and move closer to creating a happy place for our furry babies.

Digital Marketing Team

Responsibilities:

  1. Developing and implementing our Social Media strategy and increase our online presence.
  2. Managing and overseeing social media content.
  3. Measuring the success of all social media campaigns.

Field Work

Our team of Field workers are the real heroes of our organisation, working day in and out to look after our furry friends and rescuing terrified and injured animal.

Responsibilities:

  1. assisting in rescue operations
  2. Collecting bedding, food, pet essentials and medicines for animals.
  3. taking rescued animals to the vet and being there for them throughout their treatment

Research and Development

The research and development team is the mind of Pawssible. With an excellent hold on Computer skills and curiosity to discover and know new things, the R&D department helps in understanding the audience.

Responsibilities:

  1. Overseeing the entire development process of new programs.
  2. Conducting an in depth research of given task through verified sources.

In the realm of animal welfare, there is always something more that you can do. About

We are a community of PAW- lovers working for our four legged friends, aiming to give them the good life they deserve as we strongly believe all animals deserve compassion and warmth. Like us, they too are entitled to the right to life, liberty and pursuit of happiness.

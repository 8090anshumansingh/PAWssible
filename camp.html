Our Campaigns

We at PAWssible try to help our 4 legged friends in many creative ways.We have regular events and Campaigns to reach out the masses. Our recent Campaigns have got overwhelming responses and proved a great success.We as a family, work very hard to organise such events and get support form people for our little friends.



Zindagi

Year on year, thousands of strays are left on the roads to wander in search of food, shelter and water. According to the World Health Organization, in 2018, there were more than 35 million strays out on the roads. This figure has been growing each day as these furry guys aren’t spayed or neutered. Millions of them wander the roads, and it’s a danger for their lives, especially during the night times. Often at night car drivers run over these strays as they are hard to see during the night.

Isn’t it such a horrible sight when you see a puppy dead on the road? Have you ever noticed how when a puppy dies, other dogs scatter all around it crying and wailing? This is a plight we don’t notice. This is a sadness that we might not understand.

We at Pawsibble couldn't stand it and decided to run a project called ZINDAGI. It was a fundraiser event for radium collars for stray animals. It’s not just a stray, it’s a precious life that needs to be saved. We might not be able to keep all these puppies out of the streets. However, we can play a vital role in saving their precious lives.

This radium dog collar can help in identifying dogs, especially during the night. The radium collar gets activated by sunlight and shines bright around the dog’s neck during the night time. A dog collar you donate can save a voiceless creature’s life. Each collar costs around 60 INR, and for the same we had organised an open mic competition on our instagram page(https://instagram.com/pawssible__?igshid=1wfsmcn8aaxml ) to raise money for the same

Radium collars are useful in helping us see the dogs at night. The radium reflects off their collars when headlights and lights fall on them. This allows drivers to spot dogs. Radium dog collars look similar to an ordinary dog collar. This radium is reflective and helps in spotting stray dogs. Many cyclists and construction workers wear reflective clothes to appear as visible as they can on the road.Something as simple as a radium collar can help these puppies live a long life.

Mithaas

On 30th October 2020, a few days before Diwali our team at Pawsibble decided to manufacture homemade chocolates aiming to help the voiceless creatures. The purpose was to spread joy and a sense of responsibility in people. one can enjoy the taste and hygiene of homemade chocolates and at the same time the money spent can help our furry friends in a lot of ways.We posted our chocolates online for people to buy, and to our surprise we got an amazing response.

Our chocolates were wrapped as different crackers symbolising that Diwali can be more about humanity than pollution.With the great level of response that our team received from everyone, we decided to continue making chocolates and the funds that we receive for the same will be used in different animal welfare programmes

We offer different shapes and flavours of chocolates to meet everyone's needs . The success of this sweet journey made our team overwhelmed and we decided to call this fundraiser as MITHAAS.

Opt to Adopt

There are not enough homes for all the animals that are born every year. Adopting a stray helps in giving these less fortunate animals a second chance at life. We have so many animals waiting for someone to take them home. Recently team Pawssible started its " Opt To Adopt" campaign

Through our campaign we helped many innocent souls in receiving their forever home.Be The heart that chose an animal that needed a home rather than an animal that was created for homes.

About

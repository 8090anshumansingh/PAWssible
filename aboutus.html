Who Are We?

Pawssible is an Ayodhya based registered NGO spreading happiness since August 2020. It started when a small group of people decided to do their bit to make the lives of stray animals better instead of turning a blind eye to their helpless state. It started as a feeding drive but now we are doing everything we can, from rescuing animals to finding them permanent home.

We are a community of PAW- lovers working for our four legged friends, aiming to give them the good life they deserve as we strongly believe all animals deserve compassion and warmth. Like us, they too are entitled to the right to life, liberty and pursuit of happiness.

What we do?

  • Save lives: We are dedicated to rescuing, treating, rehabilitating and caring for all animals.
  • Support Community: We establish a voice for the animals through a dedicated youth that focuses on respect and compassion for all living beings.

Our Commitment

  • Promoting kindness and encouraging others to love and care for animals.
  • Ensuring Protecting and Welfare of animals by every means PAWssible with the help of our veterinary and welfare team.
  • Contributing towards Improving the health status of stray or abandoned animals through best medical treatment and vaccinations.
  • Organising fundraisers to stay true to our objectives and to ensure help to every furry friend in need !!
